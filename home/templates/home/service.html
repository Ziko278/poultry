Services That We Offer

Animal Feed Supply

Animal feeds are essential for the health and well-being of livestock and other animals. We supply all types of poultry feeds and supplements that animals need to maintain good health, grow, and produce meat, milk, or eggs. We supply hay, silage, grains, vitamin and mineral supplements. we supply the feed based on the animal's age, weight, and nutritional needs, as well as the intended use of the animal.

Live Chicken Supply

Breeding and veterinary care are both essential components of animal husbandry, whether for livestock or pets. Breeding involves selecting and mating animals with desirable traits in order to improve the quality of the offspring. This can involve careful consideration of factors such as genetics, health, and temperament. Breeding can be used to produce animals with improved productivity, disease resistance, and other desirable traits.
